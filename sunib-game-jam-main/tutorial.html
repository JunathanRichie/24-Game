24 In A Rush

How To Play

Your goal is to use all 4 given numbers and basic arithmetics (+ - x and /) to get 24 as fast as you can. To play the math 24 game on this site or on the apps we built, you don't ever need parenthesis. If you found a solution for (a,b,c,d) as a x (b - c) + d, you simply click b,-,c,x,a,+,d in that order. Our game will combine the intermediate result into one number and you never need to input a parenthesis.
